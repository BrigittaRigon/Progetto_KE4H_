CONCLUSIONS

Throughout this project, we explored the use of SPARQL queries, ArCo, and Large Language Models (LLMs) to improve the quantity and quality of information about the entities of the principal Gates in Bologna. Even though the Gates are all of the same importance, they were described very differently in the arco ontology. Therefore, it made sense to enrich them all to try and bring them to similar levels of description. Here are all the potential triples we created to enrich the knowledge graph for each entity:

What we learned

1.To make queries to investigate a KG and find relevant information; 2.To design triples by using SPARQL CONSTRUCT and LLMs; 3.To prompt LLMs and the importance of verifying and evaluating their outputs; 4.To create a website reporting the details and results of our project; 5.To think creatively and problem solve in front of something we are not familiar with The project demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence and human intelligence can work together to enrich a KG like Wikidata.
